About LATC

Luxury Africa Technology Center Ltd (LATC) is a Rwanda-based engineering and technology company specializing in automotive innovation, embedded systems development, intelligent mobility technologies, and engineering research & development.

The company operates under RDB registration and focuses on the design, development, integration, and advancement of next-generation technology solutions for the automotive sector.

LATC is committed to building African technological capability through innovation, engineering excellence, research, and proprietary technology development.

Founder & Leadership

Founder & Chief Executive Officer Jean Bosco Ombeni Mukunzi leads Luxury Africa Technology Center Ltd with a vision of building a globally recognized African automotive technology and engineering company.

His leadership focuses on automotive innovation, engineering research, embedded systems, intelligent mobility, telematics technologies, road safety solutions, and proprietary technology development.

Under his direction, LATC is committed to developing African-built technologies that contribute to safer, smarter, and more connected mobility systems across Africa and beyond.
