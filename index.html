LATC Luxury Africa Technology Center Ltd

About LATC

Luxury Africa Technology Center Ltd (LATC) is a Rwanda-based engineering and technology company specializing in automotive innovation, embedded systems development, intelligent mobility technologies, and engineering research & development.

The company operates under RDB registration and focuses on the design, development, integration, and advancement of next-generation technology solutions for the automotive sector.

LATC is committed to building African technological capability through innovation, engineering excellence, research, and proprietary technology development.

Core Focus Areas

Automotive Technology

Advanced automotive technology solutions designed for modern vehicle ecosystems.

Embedded Systems

Design and integration of intelligent embedded hardware and firmware solutions.

Vehicle Telematics

Research and development of OBD-II telematics and connected vehicle technologies.

Research & Development

Engineering research focused on innovation, performance, security and intelligent mobility.

Intellectual Property & Innovation

LATC develops proprietary technologies and engineering solutions through continuous research and innovation.

Certain technologies developed by LATC are protected through trade secret protection, intellectual property rights, confidential engineering processes, and internal innovation programs.

To protect the integrity and commercial value of proprietary innovations, technical implementation details are not publicly disclosed.
